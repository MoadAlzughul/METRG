Mohammed Anazi is a nuclear fuel analysis engineer atFramatome. He earned his PhD in Mechanical Engineering fromWashington State University in 2018. His PhD research focuses oncomputational mechanics and advance materials, where the title ofhis dissertation was:AContinuumModelofDeformationandDamageinMetalsCoatedwithNanolaminateMetallicSystemsforApplicationsinHighEnergyEnvironments. He earned his M.S.degree in Mechanical Engineering from Washington StateUniversity in 2012 on a research primarily focused on 3D printingand CAD systems. Before that, He received his B.S. degree inMechanical Engineering Technology from Central WashingtonUniversity in 2010.
