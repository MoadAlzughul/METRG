WHO We ARE

Mission

Aimed to offer novel research ideas with available capabilities to solve complex problems that can be faced with academic and industrial life. By collaborating with well-known research groups at highly prestigious academic and industrial institutions across the world. The group's research stands at the border between engineering materials science, physical chemistry, applied mathematics, and computer simulation.

Icon

Vision

The METRG aspires particularly to creating a suitable climate of collaboration between industrial and academic researchers. The group works to develop and use computational methods to understand, predict, and improve the performance of materials for energy applications.

Icon

Values

Integrity and Ethics: Integrity is a core value that defines our nonprofit organization and our work. METRG strives to do the right thing always, acts truthfully, and committed to doing what is best for our collaborators and friends.
Respect: METRG acts with transparency, honesty, integrity, and empathy. Our team focus on putting ourselves in the shoes of others
